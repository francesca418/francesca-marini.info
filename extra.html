This is a fun page to talk about some things I enjoy :)


My family is very close and important to me. We are Italian, and I love to learn how to cook traditional meals from my relatives.

In my free time, I love to get together with friends for movie & game nights, concerts & performances, family dinners, & late night conversations.

I LOVE to read ... everything. I keep a detailed catalogue of my personal library, which I store alphabetically. (Don't ask me to pick a favorite.)

Listening to podcasts is one of my favorite things to do to relax. Some of my favorite pods include Cyber, Hidden Brain, Freakonomics, More Perfect, Planet Money, Radiolab, Reply All, Science Vs., TED Radio Hour, This American Life, and 538 Politics.

I enjoy taking walks outdoors, especially to the beach, and I also dabble in iPhone photography. (Most of the pictures on this site were ones I took out on walks.) Sometimes I also rollerblade and go hiking.

I love the visual arts, and one of my favorite things to do in Philly is visiting the Philadelphia Museum of Art. I am also fairly advanced at knitting, and I've taken up embroidery during quarantine.

I am very interested in ethics, specifically as it relates to new technologies. Questions regarding ethical AI and algorithms, rights to privacy, the regulation of big tech corporations, public health, the environment, and big data fascinate me.

Some of my favorite films are Avengers: Infinity War, Casablanca, Ferris Bueller's Day Off, Get Out, The Godfather (Parts I & II), Good Will Hunting, the Harry Potter films, Little Women, the Lord of the Rings films, Parasite, Pride & Prejudice, The Shining, and Silence of the Lambs.

Some of my favorite shows include Better Call Saul, Black Mirror, Breaking Bad, House MD, Game of Thrones, The Good Place, Law & Order (the original, not the spinoffs), The Office, Stranger Things, and Watchmen.

I support Boston sports teams, and I love learning about the strategy behind sports.

Thanks for checking out my site, and don't hesitate to reach out!
